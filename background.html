Our Story: The Big Back Inn Legacy

Welcome to Big Back Inn, where flavor meets nostalgia and comfort food reigns supreme. Our story began with a simple idea: to bring back the joy of hearty, honest meals that feel like home—served fast, fresh, and always with a smile.

How It All Started

Back in 1998, in a small Midwestern town with more grain silos than stoplights, our founder, Mason “Big Mac” Beckett, had a dream. A former trucker with a big appetite and a bigger heart, Mason wanted to create a place where anyone—from long-haul drivers to local families—could find satisfying food and warm hospitality at a fair price.

He converted an old roadside inn—nicknamed the “Big Back Inn” by locals—into a cozy, no-frills eatery. What started with burgers, hand-cut fries, and creamy shakes quickly became a local legend.

Our Purpose

At Big Back Inn, we're on a mission to bring back the good stuff—classic comfort food made with real ingredients, served in a place that feels like you’ve been there a hundred times, even if it’s your first visit. We believe in food that fills you up, not just your stomach, but your soul too.

What We Stand For

Quality Without Pretension: Fresh-made burgers, hand-cut fries, and thick milkshakes—simple, real, and satisfying.

Community First: We support local farms, hire locally, and serve each guest like family—just how Mason would've wanted it.

Old-School Service, New-School Speed: Fast food, slow values—that’s the Big Back way.
